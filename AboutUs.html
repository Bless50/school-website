ABOUT US

ABOUT US

Background histroy

INIESAT (International Institute of Engineering Science Art and Technology) is located at Chefferie de Ngong, 200m from Carrefour Ebanda Simbock. Authorized by the Ministry of Higher Education in August 2019, INIESAT is a professional private university offering over 95 specialties across five different schools.

INIESAT was founded by Dr. GERALDINE NDIP, a visionary leader dedicated to advancing education in engineering, science, art, and technology. Under Dr. GERALDINE NDIP's leadership, INIESAT has grown into a prestigious institution known for its commitment to academic excellence, innovation, and community service.

Our Mission

At INIESAT, our mission is to provide a world-class education that combines engineering, science, art, and technology to foster innovation, critical thinking, and professional excellence. We aim to equip our students with the knowledge and skills necessary to make a significant impact in their respective fields and contribute positively to society.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a leading nation institution recognized for excellence in education, research, and community engagement. We strive to create an inclusive and dynamic learning environment that inspires students to achieve their full potential and become leaders in their industries.

Our Core Values

Excellence

We strive for excellence in all aspects of education, research, and service delivery, maintaining high standards in everything we do.

Innovation

We foster creativity and innovative thinking, encouraging our students to explore new ideas and solutions to real-world challenges.

Integrity

We uphold the highest standards of ethics, honesty, and transparency in all our academic and professional endeavors.

Accreditations and Affiliations

Partnership with Montana Tech University
Our institution proudly partners with Montana Tech University, a leader in engineering and technology.

    Benefits to Students
  • Acceses to Resources: Enhanced research facilitis and technology.
  • Exchange Programs: Opportunities to study abroad and experience diverse cultures.
  • Joint Programs: Globally recognized qualifications through collaborative degrees.
  • Networking: Connections with professionals and academics.
  • Internships: Increased employability through enhanced internship opportunities.

Partnership with the University of Douala

We are excited to partner with the University of Douala, one of Cameroon’s top institutions. This collaboration enhances educational opportunities through joint programs, research initiatives, and student exchanges. Together, we aim to foster innovation and prepare students for global challenges, contributing a brighter future for both communities.
