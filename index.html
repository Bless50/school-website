About us

INIESAT(international institute of engineering science art and technology) is located at chefferie de Ngong , 200m from carrefour Ebanda simbock. INIESAT is a professional private University authorized by the ministry of higher education in August 2019 with over 95 specialties, five different schools

We are excited to partner with the University of Douala, one of Cameroon’s top institutions.
